Cairo

Cairo has the highest population out of all of the cities in Egypt. Inhabiting 10 million people, the populaton of Cairo is 1.5 million units higher than New York City. Set on the Nile River, Cairo is the heart of production, finance economics in Egypt.

Giza

Giza is an Egyptian city close to Cairo. With a population of around 9 million, it is the second most populated city in Egypt. Giza is home to three large pyramids which were built by ancient Egyptians including, The Great Pyramid, close by lays the Sphinx.

Faiyum

Historically, Faiyum Egypt was the center of royal Pyramid and tomb building. Today, the population reaches up to 4 million people. The city also has the earliest evidence of farming in Egypt. Faiyum is the oldest city in Egypt and one of the oldest cities in all of Africa.
